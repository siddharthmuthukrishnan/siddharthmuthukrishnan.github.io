Research

Our best physics tells us we live in a strange sort of world. Does the fact that we live in such a world have consequences for our philosophy? And can our philosophy contribute to cutting-edge physics? I think the answer is yes and yes—and in my research I try to articulate how.

  • AOS: Philosophy of Physics, Philosophy of Science, Philosophy of Language
  • AOC: Philosophy of Computer Science, Early Modern Philosophy, Biomedical Ethics
