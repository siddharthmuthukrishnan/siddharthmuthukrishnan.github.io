About

I am a postdoctoral scholar at the Black Hole Initiative at Harvard University. Previously, I was a postdoctoral scholar at the Department of History and Philosophy of Science, University of Pittsburgh and I did a PhD at the same department (both supervised by David Wallace). I primarily work in philosophy of physics, philosophy of science, and philosophy of language. I'm also interested in early modern philosophy (especially Newton and Descartes) and epistemology. Before entering philosophy, I did a PhD in quantum computing theory.
